Data Analysis on E-commerce Sales

Project Summary

This report provides an in-depth analysis of the e-commerce fashion sales performance for the year 2022. It includes an overview of the clothing industry's growth, key trends, and an analysis of the company's sales performance throughout the year. The data and insights presented here aim to assist in understanding market dynamics, identifying areas of success, and formulating strategies for future growth. 

I will perform an analysis on the following key points:

  • Create a chart to compare the sales and orders on a monthly basis.

  • Which category of clothing has the highest sales?

  • Which E-commerce channels have the maximum sales?

  • Highest sales and order month.

  • Top States having the highest sales contribution.

  • Compare age and gender based on their orders

  • Order status over the entire year.

Dataset Overview

The dataset used for the E-commerce Sales Report contains information related to online monthly sales transactions, customer and product details, States, Order channels, and fashion categories for the year 2022.

Data Cleaning Process

Data cleaning is crucial in preparing an E-commerce Fashion Sales Report in Excel. It involves identifying and rectifying errors, inconsistencies, and missing information in the dataset to ensure accurate and reliable analysis. Here are some essential data-cleaning steps for an e-commerce fashion sales report in Excel: 

  • Removing Duplicate Entries: Identify and eliminate any duplicate records in the dataset, as they can skew the analysis and misrepresent sales figures.

  • Handling Missing Values: Address missing data in relevant columns, such as customer ID, age, date, product details, or sales figures. 

  • Data Formatting: Ensure consistent data formatting throughout the dataset. For example, standardize date formats, convert text to uppercase or lowercase, and maintain consistent units 

  • Correcting Inconsistent Data: Verify and rectify any inconsistencies in data, such as incorrect product names, categories, or prices, to ensure accuracy in the analysis.

Useful Insights

  2. Females demonstrate a higher chance of purchasing compared to males.
